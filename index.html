Our Agricultural Journey

From humble beginnings to modern farming excellence

Kyle & Brandi McConnell

At M77 Ag, our story is rooted in the rich soil of agricultural tradition and innovation. Kyle and Brandi McConnell founded M77 Ag with a vision to combine time-tested farming practices with modern agricultural technology.

Our journey began with a passion for the land and a commitment to sustainable farming practices. Over the years, we've built our reputation on quality crops, reliable service, and deep connections within the agricultural community.

Today, M77 Ag represents our dedication to excellence in farming, focusing on what we do best - growing quality crops and providing exceptional custom farming services to our neighbors and partners.

Through our partnership with ToGo Ag, we've expanded our reach while maintaining our core focus on the farm and the crops we're passionate about growing.
